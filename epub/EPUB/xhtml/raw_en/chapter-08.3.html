Final stage of the Great Patriotic War. The USSR victory in the war

At the final stage of the war (January 1944 - May 1945),the Red Army completed the defeat of the enemy in the territory of the USSR and proceeded to the liberation of European countries. On June 6, 1944 , the allies of the USSR opened the Second Front in Europe.

In April 1945, there began the final operation – the Battle of Berlin . The operation was led by Marshals G.K. Zhukov , K.K. Rokossovsky and I.S. Konev .

On May 1, 1945, Soviet soldiers M.A. Yegorov and M. V. Kantaria hoisted the flag of victory over the German Reichstag (the building of the German parliament). On May 8, 1945, an Act was signed in which Germany admitted its defeat. May 9 is Victory Day in Russia.

With the cessation of hostilities against Nazi Germany, the Great Patriotic War ended.

In the Great Patriotic War, the Soviet Union lost more than 27 million people. During the war, about thirty percent of the country's national wealth was destroyed.

The victory in the Great Patriotic War was of historical significance for the peoples of all countries and continents.

It was the Soviet Union that made a decisive contribution to the victory. In the fight against fascism, the Soviet people defended their national independence and territorial integrity. Therefore, for the USSR, the war was fair - it was the liberation war .

The main source of the victory was friendship of peoples and heroism of the nations of all the USSR republics. For the feats on the fronts of the Great Patriotic war, more than 11 thousand Soviet soldiers and officers were awarded the title of the Hero of the Soviet Union. Marshal G.K. Zhukov became three times Hero of the Soviet Union.

In August 1945, the Red Army defeated Germany’s last ally - the Japanese (Kwantung) army and liberated Manchuria and North Korea. On September 2, 1945 ,the Second World War ended.

After the war, there was formed a new system of international relations. New state borders were established in Eastern Europe. In order to maintain peace,there was created the United Nations (UN) . The USSR became a permanent member of the UN.
