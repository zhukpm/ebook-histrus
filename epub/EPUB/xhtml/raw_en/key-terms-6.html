Basic facts (lesson 6)

Basic concepts

Capitalism - socio-economic system that is based on private ownership and use of hired labor by capital (capitalism replaces feudalism).

Peasant community - form of joint activity of peasants, which is based on self-government and economic cooperation. In a community, there were combined individual ownership of the house and land and communal ownership of arable land, pastures, and forest. It was a community that was the owner of the whole land.

Landowner - large landholder (nobleman), who owned an estate (land in a certain territory).

Autocracy - monarchical form of government in Russia based on strong, almost unlimited power of the tsar.

Socialism - according to Marxist theory, this is a system that should replace capitalism. Under socialism, private property is to be destroyed; public property and social justice are to be established.

Strike - stoppage of work at an enterprise by its employees in order to force entrepreneurs to fulfill their requirements (to increase wages, to reduce working hours, etc.).
