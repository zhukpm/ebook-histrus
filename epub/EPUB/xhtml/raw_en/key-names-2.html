Basic facts (lesson 2)

Basic names

Dmitry Donskoy ((1350-1389) - Grand Duke of Moscow (from 1359) and of Vladimir (from 1362 intermittently).

Ivan III (1440-1505) - Grand Duke of Vladimir and Moscow (from 1462), sovereign of all Russia (from 1485).

Vasily III (1479-1533) - Grand Duke of Vladimir and Moscow, sovereign of all Russia (from 1505). He was the son of Ivan III and Sophia Palaiologina.

Ivan IV (Terrible) (1530-1584) - Grand Duke of Moscow and All Russia (from 1533), the first Russian Tsar (from 1547). He was the son of Grand Duke Vasily III and Princess Elena Vasilyevna Glinskaya.

Andrei Rublev (circa 1360-1370 - circa 1430) -Russian painter. In1425-1427 he painted the Holy Trinity Church in the Trinity Monastery; its main masterpiece is the icon “The Trinity” (State Tretyakov Gallery).

Ivan Fyodorov (1510-1583) - founder of printing in Russia and Ukraine. In 1564, Ivan Fyodorov, together with Pyotr Mstislavets, published “The Apostle”, which became the first Russian printed book.
