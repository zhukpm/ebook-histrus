Key dates
events

1801–1825 - the reign of Alexander I.

June 12, 1812 - Napoleon's invasion of Russia.

August 26, 1812 - the Battle of Borodino.

December 14, 1825 - the Decembrist uprising in St. Petersburg.

1825–1855 - the reign of Nicholas I.

1853–1856 - the Crimean War.

1855–1881 - the reign of Alexander II.

1861 - the abolition of serfdom in Russia (the great reform of Alexander II).
