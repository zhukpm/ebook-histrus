Basic facts (lesson 9)

Key dates and
events

1953–1964 - N.S. Khrushchev’s rule of the USSR.

1957 - launch of the artificial Earth satellite in the USSR.

April 12, 1961 - Yu.A. Gagarin’s flight into space.

1964–1982 - L.I. Brezhnev’s rule of the USSR.

July-August 1980 - the XXII Summer Olympic Games in Moscow.

1985–1991 - M.S. Gorbachev - the leader of the USSR; the First President of the USSR.

December 1991 - the collapse of the USSR. The creation of the Commonwealth of Independent States (CIS).
