Basic facts (lesson 5)

Basic concepts

Classicism (from Latin classicus - exemplary) -artistic style in European literature and art in the XVII - early XIX centuries.

Serfdom - form of feudal dependence of peasants, their attaching to the soil, the prohibition to leave those lands, to sell and acquire land, as well as their complete submission to the administrative and judicial power of the master (landowner).

Paris Peace Treaty of 1856 - completed the Crimean War. The Black Sea was declared neutral, i.e. Russia and the Ottoman Empire could not have navy and coastal fortifications there.

Romanticism (French romantisme) -ideological and artistic trend in European spiritual culture of the late XVIII - the first half of the XIX century. Romanticism is characterized by protection of freedom, sovereignty and interests of an individual.
