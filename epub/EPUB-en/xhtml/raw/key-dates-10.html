Basic facts (lesson 10)

Key dates and
vents

June 12, 1990 - signing of the Declaration of State Sovereignty of the RSFSR.

1991–1999 - President B.N. Yeltsin’s rule.

1992 - President B.N. Yeltsin’s signing of the Federal agreement with the constituent entities of the Federation.

October 3-4, 1993 - constitutional and political crisis in Russia.

December 12, 1993 - adoption of the Constitution of the Russian Federation. The first elections to the State Duma.

1993 - the Charter of the CIS was adopted.

1996 - accession of the Russian Federation to the Council of Europe.

1999 - signing of the agreement on the formation of the Union State of Russia and Belarus.
