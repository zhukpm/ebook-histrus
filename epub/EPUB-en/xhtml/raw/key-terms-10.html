Basic facts (lesson 10)

Basic concepts

Price liberalization - renunciation of the state price regulation.

The International Monetary Fund (IMF) - specialized organization of the United Nations. It was founded on December 27, 1945. It provides short- and medium-term loans to states on certain conditions aimed at improving the situation. The IMF also provided loans to the Russian Federation for carrying out the market reforms.

Privatization - form of property transformation by transferring (full or partial) state (municipal) property to private hands.

Market economy - economic system in which there are different forms of ownership, free enterprise. The whole process of economic relations is regulated by the market.

The Council of Europe - international European organization. It was established in 1949; it develops cooperation between all European countries in the field of human rights protection, democratic development, cultural interaction.
