Basic facts (lesson 4)

Key dates
events

1682–1725 - the reign of Peter I.

1700–1721 - the Great Northern War (the war between Russia and Sweden).

1703 - the foundation of St. Petersburg.

1725 - the foundation of the Academy of Sciences.

1755 - the foundation of Moscow University.

1762–1796 - the reign of Catherine II.

1783 - the issuing of the decree of Catherine II on the annexation of the Crimean Peninsula to Russia.

1785 - the issuing of the Charter to the nobility and the Charter to the towns.
