Basic names

Mikhail Sergeyevich Gorbachev (born in 1931) - statesman and political figure, General Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee (1985-1991), Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR. In 1990-1991 - President of the USSR.

Boris Nikolaevich Yeltsin (1931-2007) - statesman and political figure, Chairman of the Supreme Soviet of the RSFSR (1990-1991). In 1991-1999 â€“President of the Russian Federation.

Sergey Pavlovich Korolev (1906-1966) - Soviet scientist, designer and organizer of the production of space rocket technology and missile weapons of the USSR. General designer of space rockets. Academician of the Academy of Sciences of the USSR (1958).

Nikita Sergeyevich Khrushchev (1894-1971) - statesman and party figure; First Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee (1953-1964).In 1958-1964 - Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR.

Yuri Alekseyevich Gagarin (1934-1968) - Soviet cosmonaut, the first cosmonaut in the world, Hero of the Soviet Union.

Valentina Vladimirovna Tereshkova (born in 1937) - Soviet cosmonaut, the first woman-cosmonaut in the world, Air Force Major-General (1995).

Alexander Isayevich Solzhenitsyn (1918-2008) - Russian writer, public and political figure of the second half of the XX century. He lived and worked in the USSR, Switzerland, the USA and Russia. The author of the works - "The First Circle", "Cancer Ward", "The Gulag Archipelago" and others. Nobel Prize winner in Literature (1970).
