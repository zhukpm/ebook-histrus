Basic facts (lesson 8)

Key dates and events

September 1, 1939 - Germany’s attack on Poland. The beginning of the Second World War.

June 22,1941 - Nazi Germany attacked the USSR. The beginning of the Great Patriotic War.

September 30 - December 6, 1941 - the Battle of Moscow.

July 17, 1942 - February 2, 1943 - the Battle of Stalingrad.

July 5 - August 23, 1943 - the Battle of Kursk.

May 9, 1945 - оthe end of the Great Patriotic War. Victory Day.

September 2, 1945 - the defeat of Japan. The end of the Second World War.
