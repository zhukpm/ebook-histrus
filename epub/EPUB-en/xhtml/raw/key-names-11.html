Basic facts (lesson 11)

Basic names

Vladimir Vladimirovich Putin (born in 1952) - Russian statesman and political figure, Chairman of the Government of the Russian Federation (1999-2000); President of the Russian Federation (2000-2008); Chairman of the Government of the Russian Federation, Chairman of the United Russia Party (2008-2012). Since 2012 - President of the Russian Federation.

Dmitry Anatolyevich Medvedev (born in 1965) - Russian statesman and political figure. In 2008-2012 - President of the Russian Federation. In 2012-January 2020 - Chairman of the Government of the Russian Federation. Since 2012 - Chairman of the United Russia Party. Since January 2020 - Deputy Chairman of the Security Council of the Russian Federation.

Mikhail Vladimirovich Mishustin (born in 1966) - Russian statesman and political figure, economist.In 2006-2008 - Head of the Federal Agency for Management of Special Economic Zones.In 2010-January 2020 - Head of the Federal Tax Service of the Russian Federation. Since January 2020 - Chairman of the Government of the Russian Federation.

Zhores Ivanovich Alferov (1930-2019)- Soviet and Russian physicist, academician, winner of the 2000 Nobel Prize in Physics for the development of semiconductor heterostructures and creation of fast optoelectronic and microelectronic components.

Valery Abisalovich Gergiev (born in 1953) - one of the leading conductors of the world, art director and director of the Mariinsky Theater (St. Petersburg), People's Artist of Russia (1996).Hero of Labor of the Russian Federation (2013).

Ilya Sergeyevich Glazunov (1930-2017) - Soviet and Russian artist. Founder and former rector of the Russian Academy of Painting, Sculpture and Architecture. People's Artist of the USSR (1980).Academician of the Russian Academy of Arts (2000).Full Cavalier of the Order "For Merit to the Fatherland". Author of numerous works on the history and culture of Russia ("Rus", "Mystery of the XX century", "Kulikovo Field", "Eternal Russia", etc.).

Elena Gadzhievna Isinbaeva (born in 1982) - Russian pole vaulter. Two-time Olympic champion (2004, 2008), winner of the 2012 Olympic Games bronze medal. Three-time outdoor world champion and four-time indoor world champion. Holder of 28 world records in pole vaulting among women.

Nikita Sergeyevich Mikhalkov ((born in 1945) - Soviet and Russian actor, film director, screenwriter and producer, People's Artist of the RSFSR. Chairman of the Russian Cinematographers' Union(since 1998).He won the Academy Award (1994) for Best Foreign Language Film("Burnt by the Sun").

Mstislav Leopoldovich Rostropovich (1927-2007) - Soviet and Russian cellist, pianist and conductor, public figure, defender of human rights and spiritual freedom, teacher. People's Artist of the USSR (1966).Laureate of the Lenin Prize (1964), the Stalin Prize of the second degree (1951) and two State Prizes of Russia (1991, 1995).

Zurab Konstantinovich Tsereteli (born in 1934) - Soviet and Russian artist and sculptor. President of the Russian Academy of Arts (since 1997).People's Artist of the USSR (1980).Academician of the Academy of Fine Arts of the USSR (1988).Hero of Socialist Labor (1990).Full Cavalier of the Order "For Merit to the Fatherland". Author of the monument to Peter I in Moscow and other monuments
