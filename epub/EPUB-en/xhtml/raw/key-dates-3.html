Basic facts (lesson 3)

Key dates and events

1598–1605 - the reign of Boris Godunov.

1612 - the liberation of Moscow from the Poles by the Second Militia.

1613 - the Zemsky Sobor’s election of Tsar Mikhail Fyodorovich (Romanov) to the Russian throne.

1649 - the adoption of the Sobornoye Ulozheniye, the completion of serfdom establishment.

1654 - Pereyaslav Council, Ukraine’s accession to Russia.
