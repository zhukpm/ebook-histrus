Basic facts (lesson 3)

Basic concepts

Rebellion - spontaneous riot of people (the lower classes of society) who come out of control and destroy the old practices.

Intervention - violent interference of one or several states in the internal affairs of another state.

Peasant serfs - farmers who were completely dependent on feudal lords. They could not leave their land plots, i.e. they were “attached” to them.

Manufactory - large industrial enterprise based on the division of labor and manual craft techniques.

Schism - division of the Russian Orthodox Church in the middle of the XVII century as a result of the reform of Patriarch Nikon and Tsar Alexei Mikhailovich.

The Time of Troubles - period in the history of Russia from 1598 to 1613, which was characterized by natural disasters, the Polish-Swedish intervention, the most severe political, economic and social crisis.

Fair (an annual market) - annually recurring sale of goods.
