Basic concepts

Elimination of illiteracy - mass education of illiterate adults in reading and writing in Soviet Russia and the USSR.

Nationalization - transfer of land, enterprises and branches of the economy from private ownership to the ownership of a state or society.

Federation - form of government, in which the composition of a single state includes different entities (republics). They have certain independence, their own constitutions, as well as legislative, executive and judicial bodies. At the same time, federal (general) public authorities are formed; there are established common citizenship, a monetary system, etc.
