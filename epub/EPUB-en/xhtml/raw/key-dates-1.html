Basic facts (lesson 1)

Key dates and events

Late IX century - (in the chronicle) the calling of Prince Rurik. The beginning of the Rurik dynasty.

Late X century - the completion of the formation of the Old Russian state.

988 - the adoption of Christianity in Rus.

1019–1054 - the reign of Yaroslav the Wise in Kiev.

1147 - the first mention of Moscow (in the chronicle).

1237 - the beginning of the Mongol invasion of Russian lands.

1240 - the Battle of the Neva. Alexander Nevsky’s victory over the Swedes.

1242 - the Battle on the Ice. Alexander Nevsky’s victory over the Germans.
