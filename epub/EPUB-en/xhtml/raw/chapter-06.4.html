Culture of Russia

At the beginning of the XX century, important changes took place in the culture of Russia.

The education system was developing. There were more public, vocational (mining, river, railway) and commercial schools. There were established new private and state institutions and universities, above all, technical ones.

Due to the development of industry, natural and technical sciences were developing rapidly. V.I. Vernadsky laid the foundations of modern ecology. I.I. Mechnikov created the theory of immunity and developed various forms of combating infectious diseases. He became a Nobel Prize winner in medicine.

Significant success was achieved by N. E. Zhukovsky , the founder of aerodynamics. There began the rise of aircraft and automotive industries.

А.П. Чехов

Russian literature was developing. It reflected the social and psychological problems, which were touched upon in the works of A.P. Chekhov (the plays “The Seagull”, “The Three Sisters”, “The Cherry Orchard”), M. Gorky (the play “Enemies”, the novel “Mother”). In his poems S.A. Yesenin praised Russian village and peasantry.

At the same time, new literary trends appeared in poetry. Their representatives, A.A. Blok , A. Bely , M.I. Tsvetaeva , created new lyric poetry, which revealed the feelings of people. This period is called the Silver Age of Russian poetry.

In painting there remained the traditions of the academic school - in the works of I.E. Repin , A.M. Vasnetsov . At the same time, there appeared new trends in the paintings of Russian artists M.V. Nesterov , V.A. Serov , M.A. Vrubel .

They revealed people’s spiritual world.

Among the composers, it was S.V. Rachmaninov , I.F. Stravinsky , A.N. Scriabin that were very popular. F.I. Chaliapin was the world famous opera singer.

«Святая Русь». Картина М.В. Нестерова

Missing Развивалось русское театральное искусство. Самыми известными были театры оперы и балета - Большой театр в Москве и Мариинский театр в Санкт- Петербурге. Они и сегодня имеют огромную популярность в России и в мире.

The Russian ballet won international acclaim. The Russian ballet dancers performed in Europe, North and South America.

The general results of the development of Russian culture at the beginning of the XX century testify to its heyday. An important place in the works of the Russian cultural figures was occupied by the national and historical traditions. This contributed to the development of national identity. At the same time, Russian culture became the essential part of world culture.
