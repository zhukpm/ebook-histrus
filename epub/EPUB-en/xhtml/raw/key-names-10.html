Basic facts (lesson 10)

Basic names

Boris Nikolayevich Yeltsin (1931-2007) - statesman and political figure, Chairman of the Supreme Soviet of the RSFSR (1990-1991). In 1991-1999 - President of the Russian Federation.

Vladimir Vladimirovich Putin ((born in 1952) - statesman and political figure, director of the Federal Security Service of the Russian Federation (FSB).From 1999 - Secretary of the Security Council of the Russian Federation.1999-2000 - Chairman of the Government of the Russian Federation. 2000-2008 - President of the Russian Federation.2008-2012 - Chairman of the Government of the Russian Federation, Chairman of the United Russia Party. Since April 2012 - to date - President of the Russian Federation.

Ruslan Imranovich Khasbulatov (born in 1942) - politician, scientist and publicist, corresponding member of the Russian Academy of Sciences, last Chairman of the Supreme Soviet of the RSFSR.
