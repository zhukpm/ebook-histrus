Basic facts (lesson 11)

Basic concepts

Civil society - socially organized entity that consists of various public (non-governmental) organizations. With their help, citizens can express and defend their interests in relations with the state.

Corruption - deliberate use by officials of their status to obtain property and non-property benefits and advantages in any form, as well as bribery of these persons.

Faction in parliament (parliamentary faction) - association of elected deputies in the State Duma who belong to the same party or movement.
