Basic facts (lesson 11)

Questions and answers

  1. What socio-economic changes occurred in Russia in the 2000s?
    In 2000, V.V. Putin became President of the Russian Federation. At that time, the main economic task was to improve people's lives and fight poverty. There began the recovery of the Russian economy. The country began selling grain abroad. For 50 years, Russia could not trade its grain. At the same time, Russia paid off foreign debt to other countries. Salaries increased, pensions were paid regularly, etc.
  2. What impact did the reforms have on the financial situation of the Russian population?
    The financial situation of the population improved: salaries and pensions increased, there were given child allowances. The living conditions of the population of Russia improved. However, as before, one quarter of the country's population remained very poor.
  3. What did the country's leadership do to strengthen the Russian Federation?
    In the country, there were carried out military, judicial and other reforms. The state actively began to fight corruption. At that time, there began the implementation of national projects. New technologies were developed in industry and agriculture. The state programs to increase birthrate began to be implemented.A lot of financial assistance was given to the regions, especially the North Caucasus.
  4. Describe the cultural life of modern Russia.

    In the country, the reform of education is being conducted, and its quality is improving. There has begun uplift in science. Special achievements have been observed in the field of physics. Famous physicists Zh. I. Alferov, V.L. Ginzburg, A.A. Abrikosov became Nobel Prize winners.

    Theater and cinema are actively developing in modern Russia. There are well-known theater directors and film directors - P.S. Lungin, N.S. Mikhalkov (the Chairman of the Russian Cinematographers' Union) and others.

    Classical music occupies an important place in the life of Russian people. M.L. Rostropovich was a world-famous musician. Currently, widely known musicians are V. A. Gergiev, Yu. A. Bashmet, D. L. Matsuev. There are various trends and genres in the contemporary Russian art. Noteworthy are the works of the President of the Russian Academy of Arts, sculptor Z.K. Tsereteli. A great contribution to the development of painting was made by artist I.A. Glazunov.

    The state is actively engaged in the promotion of sports and healthy lifestyle. In 2014,in Sochi there were successfully held the XXII Winter Olympic Games.In 2018,Russia hosted the final of the Football World Cup.

    Traditional religions play an important role in the life of Russian people. There are many religious holidays. Among the Orthodox holidays, the most important and revered are Christmas (January 7), Epiphany (January 19), Easter. Muslims of Russia also celebrate all their most important holidays - Nowruz, Ramadan, Uraza-bairam, Kurban-bairam, etc.)

  5. What are the public holidays of modern Russia?
    In Russia, there are the following public holidays: June 12 is Russia Day (the day of the adoption of the Declaration of State Sovereignty); November 4 is the National Unity Day (it is associated with the end of the Time of Troubles in Russia at the beginning of the XVII century and with the victory of the people's militia led by K. Minin and D. Pozharsky over the Polish intervention); December 12 - the Constitution Day of the Russian Federation; January 1- New Year; February 23 - Defender of the Fatherland Day; March 8 - International Women's day; May 9 - Victory Day.
  6. Describe the successes and difficulties of the foreign policy of the Russian Federation.
    The foreign policy of modern Russia primarily protects the national interests of the state. It opposes the unilateral dominance of the USA in the world. Together with other states, Russia fights against terrorism. The Russian Federation prevented the USA military invasion of Syria. There are disagreements between Russia, the USA and the EU countries on the policy issues regarding Syria, the recognition of independence of South Ossetia and Abkhazia, the events in Ukraine. Currently, it is the development of cooperation with the CIS countries and the interaction within the BRICS and SCO that remain an important area.
