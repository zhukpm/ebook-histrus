Basic facts (lesson 11)

Key dates and events

2000–2008 - V.V. Putin’s presidency.

2008–2012 - D.A. Medvedev’s presidency.

2012 - V.V. Putin’s presidency.

2012 - Russia's entry into the WTO.

February 2014 - XXII Winter Olympic Games in the city of Sochi.

March 2014 - the Agreement on the accession of the Republic of Crimea to the Russian Federation.

May 2014 - creation of the Eurasian Economic Union.

2018 - election of V.V. Putin to the presidency.

June 2018 - Football World Cup in Russia
