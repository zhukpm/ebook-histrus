Basic facts (lesson 8)

Basic names

Georgy Konstantinovich Zhukov (1896-1974) - Soviet military commander, commander of the fronts during the Great Patriotic War. From August 1942 - First Deputy People's Commissar (Minister) of Defense and Deputy Supreme Commander. Marshal of the Soviet Union (1943).In 1955-1957 - Minister of Defense of the USSR.

Konstantin Konstantinovich Rokossovsky (1896-1968) - Soviet military commander, commander of the fronts during the Great Patriotic War. Marshal of the Soviet Union (1944).In 1945-1949 –Commander of the Northern Army Group. In 1949-1956 - Minister of National Defense of the Polish People's Republic.

Mikhail Alekseevich Egorov (1923-1975) - participant in the Great Patriotic War, sergeant, Hero of the Soviet Union. On May 1, 1945, together with Junior Sergeant M.V. Kantaria and Lieutenant A.P. Berest, he hoisted the flag of victory over the German Reichstag (the building of the German parliament).

Melito Varlamovich Kantaria (1920-1993) - participant in the Great Patriotic War, Junior Sergeant, Hero of the Soviet Union. On May 1, 1945,together with Sergeant M.A. Egorov and Lieutenant A.P. Berest, he hoisted the flag of victory over the German Reichstag.

Joseph Vissarionovich Stalin (Dzhugashvili) (1879-1953) - Soviet party and state leader, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party of the Bolsheviks (1922). During the Great Patriotic War - Chairman of the State Defense Committee, head of the Supreme High Command General Headquarters; People’s Commissar (Minister) of Defense, Chairman of the Council of People’s Commissars. Marshal of the Soviet Union (1943), Generalissimo of the Soviet Union (1945).
