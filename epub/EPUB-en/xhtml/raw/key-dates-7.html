Basic facts (lesson 7)

Key dates and events

February-October, 1917 - the Great October Socialist Revolution.

March 2, 1917 - the abdication of Nicholas II.

October 24-25, 1917 - the armed uprising of workers, soldiers and sailors in Petrograd. The Bolsheviks headed by V.I. Lenin came to power.

1918–1921 - the Civil War in Russia.

1917–1924 - V.I. Lenin at the head of the country.

December, 1922 - the formation of the USSR.

1924 - the adoption of the first Constitution of the USSR.

1924–1953 - Joseph Stalin at the head of the USSR.
